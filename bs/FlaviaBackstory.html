Flavia was born into slavery. She worked for a corrupt aristocrat in the western part of the empire and never knew her parents. Flavia often wished to be sold because she believed that anyone would be a better master. She may even be able to earn her freedom if she worked for someone else.

Flavia finally was sold. But, she was sold to one of the most ill-reputed gladiator managers in the city. Many of her first fights were at wild parties that aristocrats threw. They didn't make her fight male gladiators but instead found it amusing to have her fight groups of children and wild animals.

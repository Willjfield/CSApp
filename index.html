Entertainment is an integral part of this culture and one of the most popular forms of entertainment is gladiatorial battle. Hundreds of ampitheaters, both small and large, are built all over the empire to stage executions, beast hunts and gladiatorial fights.

Four young gladiators, scattered throughout the empire are now entering this brutal world: Flavia, the gladiatrix, Valerius, the struggling poor, Yonatan, the prisoner of war, and Marcus, the aristocrat.
